)
static

Obtains a projection of a skeleton joint in a depth camera space on the two-dimensional depth image.

Remarks
The screen coordinate origin point (0, 0) is located at the upper-left corner of the screen.
Parameters
projectionprojection parameters of users frame.
position3DThe coordinates of the skeleton joint in 3D space.
position2DTwo-dimensional coordinates obtained after projection calculation of skeleton joint.
Returns
TOPSENS_ERROR_OK if the call succeeds, otherwise refer to _TOPSENS_ERROR for the specific meaning.
