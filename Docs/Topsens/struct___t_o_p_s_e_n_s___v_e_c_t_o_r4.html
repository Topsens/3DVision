float W

When representing the ground plane equation, W is coefficient 'D' in equation Ax + By + Cz + D = 0.

When representing the projection to the image, W is the center in vertical direction. When representing the space rotation, W is the coefficient 'a' in quaternion a + bi + cj + dk.
